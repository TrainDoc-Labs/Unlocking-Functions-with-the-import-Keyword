### ⚠️ Important: Save Your Work
To ensure your progress is saved, please follow these steps once the notebook opens:

1.  Go to the **File** menu in the top left corner.
2.  Select **Save a copy in Drive**.
3.  A new tab will open with your personal copy. **Otherwise, your changes will not be saved!**

### 📂 Accessing Your Notebook Later
Once you have saved a copy, the notebook is permanently stored in your **Google Drive**. You can return to it at any time by:
*   Searching for the file name in [Google Drive](https://drive.google.com).
*   Looking in the **Colab Notebooks** folder automatically created in your Drive.
